### Overview
This skill provides access to cron job and scheduled task management operations.

### Capabilities
- **list_cron_jobs**: Lists cron jobs (Linux) or scheduled tasks (Windows).
- **add_cron_job**: Adds a new cron job (Linux only).
- **remove_cron_job**: Removes cron jobs matching a pattern (Linux only).
